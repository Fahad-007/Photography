About Me

Welcome to a.photographer, where my motto is…”Orange you glad it’s Friday?” Yes, even on a Monday. Because life should be like one big Friday, if you ask me. If there’s one thing we all keep saying the older we get it’s, “Time goes so fast. I don’t know what happened to the last week/month/year,” or one of my favorite client quotes, “The days are long but the years are short.” The special moments seem more fleeting the more life goes on. But that’s why I love photography. I get to be a ninja, capturing the moments before they have a chance to slip away, so you always have them. My mission is to create something that fills you with warm fuzzies every time you look at it, no matter how much time has passed.
